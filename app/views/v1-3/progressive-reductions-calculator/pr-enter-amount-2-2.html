What is your starting payment amount?

Your starting payment amount is the total BPS payment you would be owed in each scheme year (after all reductions and penalties).

It'll typically be the BPS payment you received in 2020 if you're claiming for a similar area of land in 2022 and 2023.

If the land you're claiming for has changed or may change, you should take the average unit values from your most recent BPS Claim Statement. Multiply these values by the revised claim area you're planning to use to get your starting amount.
