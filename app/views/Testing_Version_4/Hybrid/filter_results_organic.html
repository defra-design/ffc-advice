Check available funding and grants for your land or farm

From: Department for Environment, Food & Rural Affairs and Rural Payments Agency

Find out what funding we offer as part of the Farming and Countryside Programme. This is for land or farms in England.

You may be able to apply for one or more funds and grants if you or your land are eligible. The guidance for each fund and grant will explain where this is possible.

This service does not confirm your eligibility. You cannot get the same funding twice on a single land parcel.

You can also answer a few questions to narrow funding.
