Check how your BPS payments will reduce until 2024

Direct payments from the Basic Payment Scheme (BPS) will reduce each year from 2021 until 2024.

Use the calculator to check how your direct payments from BPS will reduce.

The calculator estimates how much you'll be paid each year after the following percentage reductions are applied:

Progressive reductions calculation
Scheme year 2021 2022 2023 2024
£30,000.00 or less 5% 20% 35% 50%
Amounts above £30,000.00 and no more than £50,000.00 10% 25% 40% 55%

The calculator should give you a result in less than 2 minutes.

You do not need to add personal information to the calculator. No personal information is collected or stored in the calculator.

There is no limit to the number of times you can use the calculator.

Delinked payments from 2024

Delinked payments will replace BPS direct payments from 2024 to 2027. [Read guidance on delinked payments and how they will be calculated].

Before you start

To get more accurate results you should add your 'starting payment amount' to the calculator.

Your starting payment amount will be the total BPS payment you would be owed in each scheme year (including any young farmer payment), after all reductions and penalties have been applied.

The calculator applies to Direct Payments in England. If you also claim payments for land in Scotland, Wales or Northern Ireland, you should exclude those parts of your claim from your starting payment amount.

Your BPS payment value will be calculated each year, once financial ceilings for the scheme are set. It will depend on the BPS entitlements and eligible area you claim in that year.

Get help
