How payments are calculated

The calculator will estimate your payments for each year based on the starting payment amount you add.

You should exclude payments for land in Scotland, Wales or Northern Ireland from your starting payment amount.

If you did not claim in any of the years then enter 0 as your amount

For your BPS claim in 2020

You should use the total of the following amounts shown on your 2020 BPS claim statement as your starting payment amount:

  • BPS 'gross value' amount
  • young farmer claim value, if relevant

This is the value of your claim before the RPA applied:

  • any penalties
  • a 5% reduction if your claim was more than €150,000

For your BPS claims in 2021 and 2022

You should use the total of the following amounts shown on your most recent BPS claim statement as your starting payment amount:

  • BPS 'gross value' amount
  • young farmer claim value, if relevant
