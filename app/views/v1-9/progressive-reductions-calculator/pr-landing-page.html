Check how your BPS payments will reduce until 2024

Payments from the Basic Payment Scheme (BPS) are reducing each year from 2021 until the end of the 2023 scheme year in England.

Defra plans to replace BPS payments with delinked payments from 2024. Delinked payments will reduce each year until payments finish in 2027. More information about reductions to delinked payments from 2025 to 2027 will be published when it's available.

Use the calculator to get an estimate of how much money you could receive each year until the end of 2024.

It should give you a result in less than 2 minutes.

You do not need to add personal information to the calculator. No personal information is collected or stored.

There's no limit to the number of times you can use the calculator.

How RPA calculate your payment

The Rural Payments Agency (RPA) will apply the following percentage reductions to:

  • BPS payments for 2021 to 2023 scheme years
  • delinked payments in 2024

Percentage reductions will:

  • apply to different portions of your payment
  • increase each year

For example, a BPS 2022 claim worth £40,000 would be reduced by a total of £8,500 to £31,500. The total reduction is made up of a:

  • 20% reduction applied to the first £30,000 (a reduction of £6,000)
  • 25% reduction applied to the next £10,000 (a reduction of £2,500)
