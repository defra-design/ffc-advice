This is an item in the Slurry Infrastructure grant. Read Slurry Infrastructure grant: guidance for applicants to find out more information about slurry storage including how to apply.

Grant contribution

£168.18 per m2 to a maximum grant of £16,818 (based on 100m2)

Where to use this item

Available for Slurry Infrastructure grant.

Specification

Construction of an impermeable concrete bunker to collect solid fraction directly from separator.

Floor constructed of at least 150mm concrete laid on 200mm consolidated hardcore. Enclosed on two sides and one end by 2m high 150mm thick concrete panel walls supported by RSC posts. All joints on floor and wall to be sealed to prevent seepage.

Floor to slope away from open end at a minimum fall of 1in 80 towards a drain. Drain to be connected to reception pit.

Bunker must be covered with an impermeable cover when in use.

Bunker constructed to relevant sections of BS5502 -50 and SSAFO 2010 regulations.

You can only apply for a grant towards a concrete bunker if you are applying for a separator.
